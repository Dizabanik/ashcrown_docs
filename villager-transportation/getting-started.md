# Getting Started

![Villager Transportation](https://cdn-raw.modrinth.com/data/vLUPqRLH/images/7e1db34002c56ad8a6f0500e812f2366560a7eaf.png)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Content</mark>

This is the Villager Transportation section.

Villager Transportation makes moving villagers much easier with camels and llamas.

It is useful for relocating employed villagers, preserving rare trades, and moving biome-specific villagers like Plains, Taiga, Jungle, or Swamp villagers.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Camels</mark>

Ride a saddled camel close to a villager.

The nearby villager mounts the camel passenger seat.

![Villager on Camel](https://cdn-raw.modrinth.com/data/vLUPqRLH/images/de38cda0d94eac521342fd13833fc472fa663d95.gif)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Llamas</mark>

Bring a carpeted llama close to a villager.

The villager mounts the llama when it gets close enough.

![Villager on Llama](https://cdn-raw.modrinth.com/data/vLUPqRLH/images/a2164cad86040f95d3db01046e8522d950e27e1b.gif)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Dismounting Villagers</mark>

Right-click the mounted villager to dismount it from camels and llamas.

This also works for villagers riding boats and minecarts.

{% hint style="info" %}
Villagers no longer teleport to you when dismounted.

Llamas also no longer gain extra speed while leashed and ridden.
{% endhint %}

{% hint style="warning" %}
Right-clicking an employed villager can open its trade screen while you try to dismount it.
{% endhint %}

## <mark style="color:orange;">How to Use</mark>

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Choose your mount

Use a saddled camel or a carpeted llama.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Approach the villager

Bring the mount close to the villager you want to move.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Transport the villager

Move the villager to the new location.

This works especially well for rare or biome-specific villagers.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Dismount the villager

Right-click the villager to remove it from the ride.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}
