# Artifacts

## <mark style="color:orange;">Overview</mark>

Artifacts are rare relics scattered across the Wilderness.

Each one has hidden properties that can change how you play.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Where to Find Artifacts</mark>

Artifacts are usually found in loot chests.

Any treasure chest in the Wilderness can contain one.

You can find them in:

* Villages
* Pillager Outposts
* Natural caverns
* Mineshafts
* Strongholds
* Nether Fortresses
* Wilderness structures
* Wilderness dungeons

## <mark style="color:orange;">Identifying Artifacts</mark>

New artifacts are unidentified.

Unidentified artifacts cannot be equipped or used.

Bring them to an Artifact Workshop to reveal their attributes.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Using Artifacts</mark>

Once identified, equip artifacts through the GEAR menu.

Open it from the Adventurer’s Journal or with:

```mcfunction
/gear
```

Choose carefully. The right artifact can shift a whole fight.
