# Cooking

## <mark style="color:orange;">Overview</mark>

Cooking adds a special Cooking Pot block with custom animation.

It lets you combine new and vanilla foods into stronger meals.

These dishes are highly filling and can grant long-lasting potion effects.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Discovering Recipes</mark>

Recipes are not unlocked by default.

You find them while exploring the Wilderness.

Most come from loot chests.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Learning Recipes</mark>

Hold the recipe in your hand.

Then right-click a Cooking Pot to learn it permanently.

Once learned, you can cook that dish whenever you have the ingredients.
