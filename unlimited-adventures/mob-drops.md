# Mob Drops

## <mark style="color:orange;">Overview</mark>

Several naturally spawning mobs can drop accessory items on death.

Mini-bosses also drop the keys needed for classic dungeons.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mini Bosses</mark>

### Skeleton Chieftain

* Bone Necklace
* Cloak of Wrath (`tier 3` only)
* Skeleton Key

### Nosferatu

* Gold Tooth
* Blood Vial (`tier 3` only)
* Dracula Key

### Desert Outcast

* Sunstone Ring
* Zephyr Veil (`tier 3` only)
* Desert Key
