# Getting Started

## <mark style="color:orange;">Content</mark>

This is the Unlimited Adventures section.

Unlimited Adventures expands survival with new exploration, combat, progression, and world content.

It adds new biomes, structures, mobs, dungeons, bosses, quests, custom items, and progression systems.

## <mark style="color:orange;">What Makes It Special</mark>

* A larger world with new biomes and structures to explore.
* Custom mobs like buffaloos, crocodiles, grizzly bears, and goblins.
* Adventure-focused progression with new challenges.
* Dungeons, bosses, quests, monsters, and custom items.
* Exclusive systems like 3D Backpacks, 3D Weapons, Ambient Sounds, Dungeons, and Adventure Pass.
* Custom menus, polished textures, and handcrafted sounds.
