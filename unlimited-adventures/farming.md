# Farming

## <mark style="color:orange;">Overview</mark>

The Wilderness adds new crops and a deeper farming loop.

That gives you new ingredients, new food options, and more reasons to explore.

## <mark style="color:orange;">New Crops</mark>

You can discover and grow four crops:

* Lettuce
* Corn
* Tomato
* Chili

## <mark style="color:orange;">Finding Seeds</mark>

Seeds are rare loot.

They appear in Wilderness chests, so exploration is the main way to unlock farming.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Growing Crops</mark>

* Plant crops in a Planter block.
* Water them so they can grow.
* Use bone meal to speed up growth.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Uses</mark>

Harvested crops feed into the Cooking system.

They unlock stronger meals and better food effects.
