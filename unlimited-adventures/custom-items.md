# Custom Items

## <mark style="color:orange;">Overview</mark>

Unlimited Adventures includes a built-in custom item system.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Workshop Schematics</mark>

Players **unlock new schematics** by finding them in chests across the Wilderness.

To learn one, **hold the schematic and click a Workshop block**.

Once learned, that schematic becomes available in the Workshop.
