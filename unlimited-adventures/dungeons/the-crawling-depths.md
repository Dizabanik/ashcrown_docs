# The Crawling Depths

## <mark style="color:orange;">Overview</mark>

The Crawling Depths is a Wilderness Dungeon hidden underground.

It mixes exploration, lore, traps, puzzles, and spider-heavy combat.

Its entrance appears below `Y:0`.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Backstory</mark>

> “Long ago, settlers carved a home in a vast cave. They built shrines, grew crops among fungi, and children laughed in the dark.
>
> Then the ground split. Spiders the size of wolves poured out, dragging settlers into the abyss. Their homes remain untouched—tables still set, toys abandoned… and whispers of laughter still echo through the tunnels.”

## <mark style="color:orange;">Dungeon Layout</mark>

The Crawling Depths contains eight large procedurally generated chambers:

* Fungal Cavern
* Flooded Catacombs
* Fungal Storage
* Labyrinthine Archive
* Silken Bridge
* Bandit Camp
* Abandoned Village
* Queen’s Lair

## <mark style="color:orange;">Features</mark>

* Underground entrance below `Y:0`
* Four unique spider enemies
* Spider eggs that hatch if disturbed
* Room-specific lore and atmosphere
* Ambient sounds tied to each space
* Riddles, traps, and combat challenges
* Loot hidden in pots, barrels, and chests
