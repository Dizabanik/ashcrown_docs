# Dungeons

## <mark style="color:orange;">Overview</mark>

Dungeons are major adventure spaces built around combat, traps, puzzles, and loot.

They are designed as progression challenges, not simple mob rooms.

> “Beneath the earth lie forgotten realms… places where shadows breathe, monsters lurk, and treasures gleam for those bold enough to claim them. Many enter—few return.”

## <mark style="color:orange;">Dungeon Types</mark>

### Locked Dungeons

These sealed realms require special keys dropped by rare mini-bosses in the Wilderness.

### Wilderness Dungeons

These focus more on riddles, traps, atmosphere, and exploration.
