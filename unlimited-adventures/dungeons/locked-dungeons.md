# Locked Dungeons

## <mark style="color:orange;">Overview</mark>

Locked Dungeons are ancient realms sealed behind special keys.

To enter one, you must first defeat a mini-boss in the Wilderness.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Mini Bosses</mark>

### Skeleton Chieftain

Found in forest biomes.

Drops the Skeleton Key.

### Nosferatu

Found high in mountain peaks.

Drops the Dracula Key.

### Desert Outcast

Found in desert regions.

Drops the Desert Key.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Dungeon Structure</mark>

Each locked dungeon has three dangerous floors.

Progression gates open only after enough enemies have been defeated.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Locked Dungeon Realms</mark>

### Abandoned Mining Site

* Forgotten Tunnels
* Overgrown Mineshaft
* Forbidden Cavern
* Boss: Miner’s Wrath

### Dracula’s Manor

* Haunted Cemetery
* Crypt
* Dracula’s Castle
* Boss: Lord Dracula

### Ancient Ruins

* Desert Canyon
* Ancient City
* Pyramid
* Boss: Pharaoh
