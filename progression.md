# Progression

## <mark style="color:orange;">How To Use This Page</mark>

This is the fastest way to decide what to do next.

Some packs are **power systems**. Some are **adventure branches**. Some are just **quality-of-life tools**.

If you want the short version:

1. Build your base and villager core.
2. Start stacking enchantments and trimmed gear.
3. Use Unlimited Adventures for early exploration.
4. Push into Yggdrasil for structure and dimension progression.
5. Enter the Deeper Dark once you can survive Ancient Cities.
6. Treat Stellarity as the late-game End route.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Phase 1: Early Survival Setup</mark>

Start with the systems that make the rest of the server easier.

### 1. Secure transport and utility

* Use [Villager Transportation](villager-transportation/getting-started.md) to move key villagers fast.
* Use [Horses Genetic](horses-genetic/getting-started.md) to test and breed better mounts.
* Use [Call Your Horse](call-your-horse/getting-started.md) once you have a named mount.

### 2. Protect yourself from bad deaths

Use [Dark Souls Death (RIP)](dark-souls-death-rip/getting-started.md) as your recovery loop.

If you die often, make soul recovery part of your route before pushing harder content.

### 3. Start upgrading gear immediately

Use all three enchantment systems as soon as you can:

* [Neo Enchant+](neo-enchant/getting-started.md)
* [Excellent Enchants](excellent-enchants/getting-started.md)
* [Advanced Enchantments](advanced-enchantments/getting-started.md)

These are not side content.

They are part of your main power curve.

### 4. Add tool trims to your regular loadout

Work through [Tool Trims](tool-trims/getting-started.md) and follow the [Tutorial](tool-trims/tutorial.md).

This gives your core tools and weapons a long-term upgrade path.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Phase 2: Build Your Core Gear</mark>

Before deep exploration, aim for a stable kit.

### Priority goals

* Good mobility enchantments
* Better mining and farming tools
* Safer ranged damage
* Reliable death recovery
* Fast villager access for enchant trading

### Best pages to check

* [Neo Enchant+ Enchantments](neo-enchant/enchantment/)
* [Excellent Enchants - Enchantments](excellent-enchants/enchantments.md)
* [Advanced Enchantments - Weapons](advanced-enchantments/weapons.md)
* [Advanced Enchantments - Armor & Movement](advanced-enchantments/armor-and-movement.md)
* [Advanced Enchantments - Tools, Fishing & Farming](advanced-enchantments/tools-fishing-and-farming.md)
* [Neo Enchant+ Non Combinable](neo-enchant/non-combinable.md)
* [Neo Enchant+ Compatibility](neo-enchant/compatibility-with-other-mods.md)

{% hint style="info" %}
If your next goal is combat, finish your enchant and trim setup before entering any custom dimension.
{% endhint %}

## <mark style="color:orange;">Phase 3: First Big Adventure Branch</mark>

Once your gear is stable, start with the safest broad progression branch.

### Recommended first branch: Unlimited Adventures

Start here:

* [Unlimited Adventures](unlimited-adventures/getting-started.md)
* [Custom Items](unlimited-adventures/custom-items.md)
* [Artifacts](unlimited-adventures/artifacts.md)
* [Mob Drops](unlimited-adventures/mob-drops.md)

Then move into:

* [Dungeons](unlimited-adventures/dungeons/)
* [Locked Dungeons](unlimited-adventures/dungeons/locked-dungeons.md)
* [The Crawling Depths](unlimited-adventures/dungeons/the-crawling-depths.md)

Why first:

* It expands the world without forcing a single hard entry gate.
* It gives loot, bosses, dungeons, and items that help every later branch.
* It builds combat confidence before harsher dimension content.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Phase 4: Midgame Dimension Progression</mark>

After your first adventure branch, move into one of the two bigger midgame paths.

### Path A: Yggdrasil

Start here:

* [Yggdrasil - Getting Started](yggdrasil/getting-started.md)
* [Structure](yggdrasil/structure/)
* [Asflors Village](yggdrasil/structure/asflors-village.md)
* [Runic Dimension](yggdrasil/runic-dimension.md)
* [The items](yggdrasil/the-items.md)
* [Reva (Pet)](yggdrasil/reva-pet.md)

Recommended route:

1. Find structures and learn the world layout.
2. Start the Asflors questline.
3. Reach the Runic Island.
4. Open the Runic Dimension.
5. Farm structure loot and unique items.

Yggdrasil is a good next step if you like exploration, structure puzzles, and guided quest-style progression.

### Path B: Deeper Dark

Start here:

* [Deeper Dark - Getting Started](deeper-dark/getting-started.md)
* [Opening the Portal](deeper-dark/opening-the-portal.md)
* [Biomes](deeper-dark/biomes/)
* [Structures](deeper-dark/structures/)
* [Items & Enchantments](deeper-dark/items-and-enchantments.md)

Recommended route:

1. Prepare for Ancient City-level danger.
2. Unlock entry with an Echo Shard.
3. Learn the safe biomes and structure layout.
4. Collect sculk items and enchantments.
5. Use the dimension for loot, utility tools, and stronger builds.

Choose this path once vanilla Deep Dark content already feels manageable.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Phase 5: Late-Game End Route</mark>

This is the hardest main progression branch here.

### Stellarity

Start here:

* [Stellarity - Getting Started](stellarity/getting-started.md)
* [Biomes](stellarity/biomes.md)
* [Structures](stellarity/structures.md)
* [Bosses & Progression](stellarity/bosses-and-progression.md)
* [Gear & Systems](stellarity/gear-and-systems.md)

Recommended route:

1. Finish your best pre-End gear first.
2. Clear the expanded Stronghold.
3. Enter the reworked End.
4. Destroy all End Crystals and defeat the dragon.
5. Unlock the Altar of the Accursed.
6. Raid End Cities and gather keys.
7. Upgrade armor, trinkets, and signature weapons.
8. Summon and defeat the Empress of Light.

Stellarity should be treated as your late-game capstone.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Do not rush Stellarity with weak gear. It expects strong enchants, good movement, and reliable ranged damage.
{% endhint %}

## <mark style="color:orange;">What To Do At Any Time</mark>

These systems fit into every phase.

### Improve movement and travel

* Breed better mounts with [Horses Genetic](horses-genetic/getting-started.md)
* Bind a summoning horn with [Call Your Horse](call-your-horse/getting-started.md)
* Move villagers where you need them with [Villager Transportation](villager-transportation/getting-started.md)

### Improve your base economy

* Use villager access for enchants and gear loops.
* Farm Unlimited Adventures drops and items.
* Keep spare combat kits for Dark Souls Death recovery runs.

### Improve your equipment constantly

Check these pages often when replacing gear:

* [Neo Enchant+](neo-enchant/getting-started.md)
* [Excellent Enchants](excellent-enchants/enchantments.md)
* [Advanced Enchantments](advanced-enchantments/getting-started.md)
* [Tool Trims Tutorial](tool-trims/tutorial.md)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Best Recommended Overall Order</mark>

If you want one simple route, follow this:

1. Base setup, villagers, horse tools, and early enchants.
2. Tool Trims and stronger utility gear.
3. [Unlimited Adventures](unlimited-adventures/getting-started.md) overworld content.
4. [Yggdrasil](yggdrasil/getting-started.md) structures and Runic Dimension.
5. [Deeper Dark](deeper-dark/getting-started.md) once Ancient Cities feel safe.
6. [Stellarity](stellarity/getting-started.md) as the late-game End branch.

## <mark style="color:orange;">If You Are Stuck</mark>

### "I die too fast"

Go back to enchants, tool trims, and safer Unlimited Adventures content.

### "I have gear but no direction"

Go to [Unlimited Adventures Dungeons](unlimited-adventures/dungeons/) or start [Yggdrasil's Runic Dimension path](yggdrasil/runic-dimension.md).

### "I want the hardest content"

Push directly toward [Stellarity Bosses & Progression](stellarity/bosses-and-progression.md), but only after finishing a real enchant setup.
