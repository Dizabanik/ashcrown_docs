# Getting Started

## <mark style="color:orange;">Content</mark>

This is the Horses Genetic section.

Horses Genetic is a tool datapack that shows your horse stats.

Use it to compare mounts, improve breeding choices, and build better studs over time.

Horse stats can improve from generation to generation. That makes selective breeding worth tracking.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Main Features</mark>

* Read your current mount stats.
* Write stats onto paper in your main hand.
* Show stats in chat or in the action bar, depending on your settings.

## <mark style="color:orange;">How to Use</mark>

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Mount your horse

Get on the horse you want to inspect.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Hold blank paper

Place `1` blank paper in your main hand.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Look at the saddle

Look straight down for about one second.

Aim at roughly `-90°`.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Read the stats

The datapack writes the stats onto the paper.

Depending on your options, it can also show them in chat or in the action bar.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}
