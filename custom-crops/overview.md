# Overview

## <mark style="color:orange;">Overview</mark>

Custom Crops expands farming with seasons, watering, crop protection, and fertilizers.

It adds more planning than vanilla farming.

![](https://nullforums.net/attachments/download-1-png.41962/)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Seasons and Greenhouses</mark>

Season is a core part of the farming system.

Crops only grow in their matching season.

In the wrong season, they enter a dead stage.

Use Greenhouse Glass to let them grow all year.

![](https://nullforums.net/attachments/download-gif.41964/)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Crows and Scarecrows</mark>

Crops have a small chance to be attacked by crows.

Place a Scarecrow to protect nearby crops.

![](https://nullforums.net/attachments/download-1-gif.41965/)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Sprinklers and Watering Cans</mark>

Sprinklers and Watering Cans work like the farming tools in _Stardew Valley_.

Sprinklers water pots on a timer and help keep their moisture up.

Watering Cans require manual use, but they give players full control.

![](https://nullforums.net/attachments/download-2-gif.41966/)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Fertilizers</mark>

There are five fertilizer types.

Each one supports crop growth in a different way.

Some increase yield.

Some help pots stay wet longer.

Use a Soil Surveyor to check the fertilizer applied to a pot.

![](https://nullforums.net/attachments/download-3-gif.41967/)
