# Amethyst Mineshaft

## <mark style="color:orange;">Gallery</mark>

![Amethyst Mineshaft Top Room](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/nHr26oc2/images/96d46985a55bf59ebe23011e9b144ad11aefadf8.png)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Information</mark>

The Amethyst Mineshaft generates in the Amethyst Mines biome.

You can locate it with an Amethyst Mineshaft Locator Compass. The structure is made of wooden mineshaft tunnels around a stone center room with a staircase to the top room. The top room has a domed ceiling, four side rooms, four housing hallways, and a sculk conversion pedestal above the staircase.

The side rooms include:

* a blast furnace room with chests
* a farm
* an animal pen
* an auto respawn anchor room with reinforced deepslate

There is a large amount of loot here.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Shockwave</mark>

A new mob called the Shockwave spawns in this structure.

One Shockwave appears on each floor except the top room. It stays on its own floor. It is loud, strong, very fast, and constantly roams.

If one gets close, get against a wall, block yourself in, and wait for it to pass.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Altar Fragments</mark>

Every floor contains one altar fragment.

You need at least one to create a sculk converter. You need eight to fully power one.
