## <mark style="color:orange;">Information</mark>

Sculk Traps act like the dimension's dungeon structures.

Each trap is a deepslate brick disk held up by four pillars. On top sits a barrel, plus two sensors and two shriekers. Loot includes enchanted books, experience bottles, and one of the first four sculk enchantments.

They generate in the Volcanic Caverns and the main Deeper Dark biome.
