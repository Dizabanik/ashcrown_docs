## <mark style="color:orange;">Information</mark>

Ancient Fortresses are massive castle-like structures found in the Deeper Dark Cavern biome.

They contain a central portal like Ancient Cities. Their walls are tall and packed with paths and rooms. Loot focuses on potions, combat gear, wood, and food.
