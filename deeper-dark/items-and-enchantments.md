# Items & Enchantments

## <mark style="color:orange;">New Items</mark>

### Splash Potion of Blindness

Lasts for 1 minute.

It can be found in Ancient Fortress chests or crafted with sculk conversion.

### Activated Sculk Shrieker

A sculk shrieker that can spawn the Warden.

It is crafted with sculk conversion.

### Laboratory Locator Compass

Points to a Laboratory.

It can be found in lower Amethyst Mineshaft barrels.

### Amethyst Mineshaft Locator Compass

Points to an Amethyst Mineshaft.

It can be found in Ancient Fortress chests.

### Ancient Fortress Locator Compass

Points to an Ancient Fortress.

It can be found in Ancient Village chests.

### Warden Tracker

When held, it shows your Warden warning level and points to the nearest Warden within 64 blocks.

It also tells you whether the Ancient Dark has noticed you. It can be found in Amethyst Mineshaft supply chests and can also be created with sculk conversion.

### Conversion Altar Fragment

Each Amethyst Mineshaft floor contains one altar fragment.

Drop one onto a sculk catalyst to create a sculk converter. Drop more onto the converter to upgrade it. A converter can hold up to 8 fragments.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Sculk Enchantments</mark>

Sculk Enchantments are special enchantments with blue text.

They can only be found in the Deeper Dark. Some come from Sculk Traps. Others come from enchanting in a sculk converter.

### Clearsight

* Protects the wearer from Blindness I.
* Reduces the fog from Darkness I.
* Applies to helmets.
* Can appear on armor in structures, in Sculk Traps, or through sculk conversion enchanting.

### Sonic Boom

* Lets the wearer use the Warden's sonic attack.
* Sneak while looking down to charge it.
* Stop sneaking to fire it.
* Only works for players.
* Applies to chestplates.
* Can appear on armor in structures, in Sculk Traps, or through sculk conversion enchanting.

### Safefall

* Increases safe fall distance.
* Has 3 levels.
* Applies to leggings.
* Can appear on armor in structures, in Sculk Traps, or through sculk conversion enchanting.

### Darkspeed

* Increases movement speed in complete darkness.
* Works at light level 0.
* Has 3 levels.
* Applies to boots.
* Can appear on armor in structures, in Sculk Traps, or through sculk conversion enchanting.

### Resonate

* Makes mobs glow after they hit you.
* Lets you block sonic boom attacks, except real Warden sonic booms.
* Applies to shields.
* Can be obtained in Sculk Traps or through sculk conversion enchanting.

### Shrieker Sense

* Highlights sculk shriekers you look at.
* Works even through walls.
* Applies to spyglasses.
* Can only be obtained through sculk conversion enchanting.
