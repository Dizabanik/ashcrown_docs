## <mark style="color:orange;">Information</mark>

The Amethyst Mines are covered in amethyst, calcite, and smooth basalt.

Calibrated sculk sensors can generate on top of amethyst blocks. That lets them chain signals into other sensors and shriekers, although sculk blocks are less common here overall. You can also find scattered mining supplies.

The only structure here is the Amethyst Mineshaft.
