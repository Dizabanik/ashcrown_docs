## <mark style="color:orange;">Information</mark>

The Deep Oasis is a calm, sculk-free biome found at the tops of tall caves.

It contains many plants and giant hanging vines. Sniffers and their plants can spawn here inside sniffer nests, which are geode-shaped caves made of moss and coarse dirt. More shriekers generate below this biome than elsewhere in the dimension.

No structures generate here.
