## <mark style="color:orange;">Information</mark>

The Ancient Dark is the original home of sculk.

This biome is a huge cavern with thin pillars and ledges. It is surrounded by an Obsidian Barrier. The terrain is converted into deep layers of sculk. Bone blocks rise from the ground and hang from the ceiling. The bottom contains lava lakes with soul sand beaches. The fog and water are tinted red.

The sculk here is more advanced. It is also touch-sensitive. Touching any type of sculk alerts the biome and starts spawning Wardens. Unique sculk blocks with unique abilities also generate here.

This is the most dangerous biome in the dimension. Laboratories are the only structures found here.
