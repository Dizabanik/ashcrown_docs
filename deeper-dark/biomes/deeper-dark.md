## <mark style="color:orange;">Information</mark>

The Deeper Dark biome is the main and most common biome in the dimension.

It is similar to the Overworld Deep Dark, but with more sculk, diamond fossils, blue fog, sculk traps, and ancient villages. All Overworld ores generate here, including emerald ore.
