# Getting Started

![Deeper Dark Logo](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/nHr26oc2/images/2bb5fb7152656ba5a01a07e44df8001d21df407e.png)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Content</mark>

This is the Deeper Dark section. Here you will find the dimension, its biomes, structures, items, enchantments, and portal mechanics.

Deeper Dark adds a new sculk-covered dimension full of treasure and danger. It adds new loot, mechanics, items, blocks, advancements, structures, enchantments, and more.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Entering the Deeper Dark</mark>

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Find the portal

Go to the portal structure in the center of an Ancient City.

Stand on the reinforced deepslate.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Hold an Echo Shard

Hold an echo shard in your hand.

Particles will appear when the portal is ready.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Enter the dimension

Step into the portal.

You will be transported into the Deeper Dark.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Leave the dimension

Find reinforced deepslate inside the dimension.

Hold your now-enchanted echo shard to leave.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

{% hint style="warning" %}
If a Warden is nearby, you cannot leave the dimension until it despawns or moves far enough away.
{% endhint %}

## <mark style="color:orange;">Dimension Rules</mark>

* If you reach the top of the dimension, a mysterious force pulls you back down.
* Resisting that force damages you.
* A special death-drop behavior exists for this dimension. It is disabled by default and can be enabled by gamerule. When enabled, deaths still drop items even with `keepInventory`, but those items cannot despawn or be destroyed.
