# Sculk Conversion

## <mark style="color:orange;">How Sculk Conversion Works</mark>

Sculk Conversion is a crafting system built around the sculk catalyst.

To create a sculk converter, drop altar fragments onto a sculk catalyst. Altar fragments are found in Amethyst Mineshafts.

Sculk converters need two resources:

* experience
* soul fire

Surround the converter with soul fire in a circle. Then kill mobs near it to feed it experience.

The converter shows:

* a green bar for stored experience
* blue flames for stored soul fire

Place the item to convert in the blue frame at the top of the altar. When the converter has the required resources, kill a mob near it. The item will convert after a short delay.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Conversion Recipes</mark>

Each recipe needs an input item, a minimum XP amount, and a minimum soul fire count.

### Echo Shard

* Input: Amethyst Shard
* XP: 1
* Flames: 0

### Sculk Catalyst

* Input: Bone Block
* XP: 10
* Flames: 4

### Experience Bottle

* Input: Awkward Potion
* XP: 5
* Flames: 1

### Activated Sculk Shrieker

* Input: Sculk Shrieker
* XP: 15
* Flames: 8

### Splash Potion of Blindness

* Input: Awkward Potion
* XP: 5
* Flames: 3

### Enchanted Echo Key

* Input: Echo Shard
* XP: 5
* Flames: 2
* The saved location is the block above the converter.
* This must be done outside the Deeper Dark.
* This creates the same kind of enchanted key used when entering the dimension.

### Reinforced Deepslate

* Input: Deepslate
* XP: 30
* Flames: 4

### Soul Sand

* Input: Sand
* XP: 1
* Flames: 1

### Soul Soil

* Input: Dirt
* XP: 1
* Flames: 1

### Warden Tracker

* Input: Compass
* XP: 30
* Flames: 1

## <mark style="color:orange;">Enchanting with the Converter</mark>

A sculk converter can also add enchantments to enchantable items.

* Place an enchantable item in the conversion slot.
* Regular books work. Enchanted books do not.
* When triggered, the converter tries to add a compatible enchantment.
* If nothing happens, it may have selected an already present enchantment or a curse.
* That does not mean the item is fully enchanted.
* Trigger it again to roll another result.
* Enchanting requires 30 XP.
* More soul fire increases enchanting power.
