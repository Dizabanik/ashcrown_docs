# Getting Started

## <mark style="color:$danger;">ALL ITEMS AND MATERIALS CAN BE FOUND</mark> [<mark style="color:$success;">HERE</mark>](https://koharasbasement.wiki.gg/wiki/Stellarity)

## <mark style="color:orange;">Content</mark>

Stellarity is a full End overhaul for Minecraft 1.20 and 1.21.

It runs as a datapack packaged like a server-side mod. Vanilla clients can join without installing it.

The End is no longer empty. It is a hostile, occupied realm shaped by Illager expansion, magic, and heavy combat.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Before You Enter</mark>

Strongholds are much larger than vanilla.

They are heavily guarded by Illagers and work like siege dungeons before the real dimension starts.

You still need Eyes of Ender to activate the portal.

{% hint style="warning" %}
Do not expect vanilla End progression. Stellarity is balanced around late-game gear and sustained combat.
{% endhint %}

## <mark style="color:orange;">What Changes In The End</mark>

* Terrain is fully reworked with large height differences, thicker islands, deep gaps, and hanging landmasses.
* The dimension adds 15 new biomes and heavily reworks the 4 vanilla End biomes.
* Outer islands generate villages, ruins, dungeons, shipwrecks, campsites, and boss structures.
* End Cities become locked combat zones with anti-building rules.
* Boss fights, loot progression, armor sets, weapons, and utility systems are all expanded.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Progression Route</mark>

{% stepper %}
{% step %}
### Clear a Stronghold

Fight through the expanded Stronghold and reach the portal room.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Enter the End

Expect dangerous terrain, biome-specific hazards, and much stronger exploration pressure.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Defeat the Ender Dragon

Destroy every active End Crystal first. The dragon is fully invulnerable while any crystal remains.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Unlock the Altar of the Accursed

After the first dragon kill, the altar awakens on the central island and opens late-game crafting.
{% endstep %}

{% step %}
### Push Into Outer-Island Content

Raid End Cities, collect keys, fish the void, upgrade gear, and prepare for the Empress of Light.
{% endstep %}
{% endstepper %}

## <mark style="color:orange;">Navigation Tips</mark>

Inside the End, Eyes of Ender change behavior.

Instead of pointing to a Stronghold, they point to the nearest End Gateway.

This helps you recover your route when exploring deep outer islands.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Main Features</mark>

### Biomes

Stellarity splits the End into barren, lively, and transitional biome types.

The Hallow, Amethyst Forest, Flesh Tundra, Prismarine Forest, and Endless Dunes are some of the most important zones.

### Structures

You can find End Villages, Campsites, Desert Ruins, Shipwrecks, End Dungeons, and the Chapel of Light.

### Bosses

The Ender Dragon is rebuilt as a multi-step fight.

The Empress of Light is an optional superboss tied to the Chapel of Light.

### Systems

Late-game progression revolves around the Altar of the Accursed, Void Fishing, Hallow Transmutation, and new trinkets.
