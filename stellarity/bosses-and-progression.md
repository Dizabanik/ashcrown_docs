# Bosses & Progression

## <mark style="color:orange;">Progression Loop</mark>

Stellarity pushes players through a clear late-game route:

1. Break into a reinforced Stronghold.
2. Survive the reworked End.
3. Beat the Ender Dragon.
4. Use the Altar of the Accursed.
5. Raid End Cities and gather keys.
6. Summon and defeat the Empress of Light.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Ender Dragon</mark>

The Ender Dragon fight is heavily reworked.

### Summoning

The first fight starts normally.

To respawn the dragon, place End Crystals on the bedrock around the exit portal, not on the portal rim.

### Main Changes

* The dragon has 300 HP.
* It has built-in armor, so weak gear performs badly.
* The boss UI tracks both dragon health and crystal status.
* The dragon perches less often than vanilla.
* Its fireball attack is replaced by a larger and more dangerous version.

### Crystal Rule

End Crystals no longer heal the dragon.

Instead, any active crystal makes the dragon completely invulnerable.

You must destroy every crystal before the boss can take damage.

### Final Stand

At 1 HP, the dragon enters a final phase.

It returns to the central portal and waits for the finishing hit.

### Rewards

Defeating the dragon can reward:

* **Life Crystal:** rapid regeneration while standing still.
* **Dragon Wings:** an Elytra variant with extra armor and toughness, but lower movement speed.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Altar of the Accursed</mark>

After the first dragon kill, the Altar of the Accursed awakens on the central island.

It becomes the main late-game crafting station.

### What It Does

* Fuses dropped items into advanced gear.
* Transfers enchantments from gear onto plain books.
* Dyes Elytra and adds custom particle trails.
* Cleanses dyed Elytra with a Water Bucket.

The Dragonblade is embedded in the altar when it appears.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Empress of Light</mark>

The Empress of Light is Stellarity's optional superboss.

She is tied to the Chapel of Light in the Hallow.

### How To Summon

Collect Starlight Soot from Pixies or related fairy sources.

Place it at the center of the Chapel of Light to begin the fight.

### Fight Style

This is a projectile-heavy bullet-hell fight.

Key attacks include:

* **Prismatic Bolts:** tracking lasers.
* **Dash:** direct high-speed ram.
* **Sun Dance:** rotating light rays.
* **Everlasting Rainbow:** lingering star projectiles.
* **Ethereal Lance:** hard-light swords fired at the player.

### Daytime Enrage

If the Empress is fought during daytime in the Overworld, she becomes massively stronger.

Most hits become effectively fatal.

A no-hit daytime win unlocks the **Terraprisma**.

### Standard Rewards

Night or standard wins can reward items like:

* Prismatic Pearl
* Prismatic Punch
* Nightglow
* Starlight
* Kaleidoscope
* Eventide
* Soaring Insignia
* Empress Wings
