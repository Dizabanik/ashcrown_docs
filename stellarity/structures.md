# Structures

## <mark style="color:orange;">Overworld Entry</mark>

Strongholds are rebuilt to match Stellarity's difficulty.

They are around 7 times larger than vanilla and are actively occupied by Illagers.

Reaching the portal chamber feels like clearing a siege zone, not exploring a ruin.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Outer-Island Structures</mark>

Stellarity expands the End with many new structure types.

### End Villages

End Villages act as local trade hubs.

They include villagers who trade dimension-specific items.

### Campsites

Small expedition remnants scattered across the outer islands.

### Desert Ruins

Ruins that commonly appear in dry biomes like the Endless Dunes.

### Shipwrecks

Broken vessels stranded far from safety.

These reinforce the idea that earlier expeditions failed here.

### End Dungeons

Combat-focused structures with higher pressure than standard overworld loot sites.

### Chapel of Light

The Chapel of Light only generates in the Hallow.

It is the summoning site for the Empress of Light.

## <mark style="color:orange;">End City Siege Rules</mark>

End Cities are no longer easy loot towers.

Inside an End City perimeter, players cannot place or break blocks.

This blocks vanilla cheesing methods like pillar climbing, tunneling, or exterior scaffolding.

To remove the restriction, climb the city and destroy the Protective Crystals at the top.

{% hint style="warning" %}
You must clear End Cities through their intended routes. Bring ranged damage, mobility, and enough healing.
{% endhint %}

## <mark style="color:orange;">End City Defenders</mark>

Shulkers are no longer a single enemy type.

End Cities use multiple color-coded Shulker variants with different attack behaviors.

This makes city fights less predictable and much more dangerous in close quarters.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Vault Keys</mark>

The best End City loot is locked behind vault systems.

### Purpur Key

Used for standard vaults.

### Gilded Purpur Key

Used for Ominous Vaults with better rewards.

### Winged Key

Used for another high-tier access layer tied to premium loot.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Why Structures Matter</mark>

Structures are the main source of keys, templates, upgrades, and progression items.

If you skip them, you will slow down armor crafting, weapon upgrades, and late-game unlocks.
