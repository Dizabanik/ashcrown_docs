# Tutorial

## <mark style="color:orange;">Tool Trim Smithing Templates</mark>

These smithing templates are used in Smithing Tables to trim weapons and tools.

Each template is found in a different structure.

### Template Locations

* ![Linear](https://imgur.com/xItrOqb.png) **Linear**
  * Structure: Trail Ruins or Mineshaft
  * Chance per chest: `7.6%` or `5.6%`
* ![Tracks](https://imgur.com/RxjzI7J.png) **Tracks**
  * Structure: Pillager Outpost or Mansion
  * Chance per chest: `37.5%` or `50%`
* ![Charge](https://imgur.com/LpdAQ3g.png) **Charge**
  * Structure: Ancient City
  * Chance per chest: `5%`
* ![Frost](https://imgur.com/xNVu2Qn.png) **Frost**
  * Structure: Igloo
  * Chance per chest: `40%`

### Duplicating Templates

Tool Trim templates can be duplicated in a crafting table.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Tool Trimming</mark>

Weapons and tools can be trimmed in a Smithing Table.

You can use 11 materials:

* ![Amethyst](https://imgur.com/dK66Usj.png) Amethyst Shard
* ![Copper](https://imgur.com/Sedgr1J.png) Copper Ingot
* ![Diamond](https://imgur.com/5xCJZQ9.png) Diamond
* ![Emerald](https://imgur.com/WJbkogZ.png) Emerald
* ![Gold](https://imgur.com/jOAXshn.png) Gold Ingot
* ![Iron](https://imgur.com/7Ch0IPt.png) Iron Ingot
* ![Lapis](https://imgur.com/wsIxsUU.png) Lapis Lazuli
* ![Netherite](https://imgur.com/JJY5pN1.png) Netherite Ingot
* ![Quartz](https://imgur.com/zwXP3Xh.png) Quartz
* ![Redstone](https://imgur.com/DMfehpQ.png) Redstone Dust
* ![Resin](https://i.postimg.cc/pr0XFSGM/resin-brick.png) Resin Brick

Combined with the Tool Trim templates, this gives more than `1700` combinations.
