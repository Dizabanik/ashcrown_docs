## <mark style="color:orange;">Tools</mark>

* **Experience** — Chance to get more experience from ores. Applies to `Tools`. Rarity: Simple. Max level: `5`.
* **Haste** — Allows you to swing your tools faster. Applies to `Tools`. Rarity: <mark style="color:purple;">Fabled</mark>. Max level: `3`.
* **Hasten** — Chance to gain Haste after breaking blocks. Applies to `Tools`. Rarity: Simple. Max level: `3`.
* **Restore** — Upon breaking, item has a chance to lose this enchantment and repair half of its durability. Applies to `Tools`. Rarity: <mark style="color:purple;">Fabled</mark>. Max level: `4`.
* **Telepathy** — Automatically places blocks broken by tools in your inventory. Applies to `Tools`. Rarity: <mark style="color:green;">Unique</mark>. Max level: `4`.
* **Unbreakable** — Tools with this enchant become unbreakable. Applies to `Tools`. Rarity: <mark style="color:purple;">Fabled</mark>. Max level: `1`.

### <mark style="color:yellow;">Weapons and tools</mark>

* **Reforged** — Protects weapons and tools durability. Items take longer to break. Applies to `Weapons and tools`. Rarity: <mark style="color:blue;">Elite</mark>. Max level: `10`.

### <mark style="color:yellow;">Weapons + tools + bows</mark>

* **Soulbound** — A chance to keep item on death. Applies to `Weapons + Tools + Bows`. Rarity: <mark style="color:purple;">Fabled</mark>. Max level: `3`.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Pickaxes</mark>

* **Multiplication** — Chance to multiply ore drops. Costs `5` souls per activation. Applies to `Pickaxe`. Rarity: <mark style="color:purple;">Fabled</mark>. Max level: `5`.
* **Rebreather** — Chance to regain air when mining under water. Applies to `Pickaxe`. Rarity: Simple. Max level: `2`.
* **Replenish** — Chance to restore food while mining. Applies to `Pickaxes`. Rarity: <mark style="color:blue;">Elite</mark>. Max level: `3`.
* **Smelting** — Chance to smelt mined blocks. Applies to `Pickaxes`. Rarity: Simple. Max level: `3`.
* **Soulminer** — Chance to get souls by mining. Applies to `Pickaxe`. Rarity: <mark style="color:purple;">Fabled</mark>. Max level: `3`.
* **Vein Miner** — Mine entire vein of ores. Applies to `Pickaxes`. Rarity: <mark style="color:blue;">Elite</mark>. Max level: `3`.

### <mark style="color:yellow;">Pickaxes and shovels</mark>

* **Trench** — Chance to break in a `3x3` area. Applies to `Pickaxes, Shovels`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `9`.

### <mark style="color:yellow;">Axes as tools</mark>

* **Timber** — Chance to break a tree in one hit. Applies to `Axes`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `3`.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Hoes and farming</mark>

* **Carrot Planter** — Plant carrots in a `3x3` area by `shift + right-clicking`. Applies to `Hoes`. Rarity: Simple. Max level: `3`.
* **Planter** — Plant seeds in a `3x3` area by `shift + right-clicking`. Applies to `Hoes`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `3`.
* **Potato Planter** — Plant potatoes in a `3x3` area by `shift + right-clicking`. Applies to `Hoes`. Rarity: Simple. Max level: `3`.
* **Replanter** — Replants crops when harvested. Applies to `Hoes`. Rarity: <mark style="color:green;">Unique</mark>. Max level: `1`.

## <mark style="color:orange;">Fishing rods</mark>

* **Auto Reel** — Automatically reels in the fishing rod when it gets a bite. Applies to `Fishing Rods`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `4`.
* **Bait** — Chance to receive double fishing drops. Applies to `Fishing Rod`. Rarity: <mark style="color:red;">Legendary</mark>. Max level: `3`.
* **Fire Hook** — The hook is on fire. Applies to `Fishing Rod`. Rarity: <mark style="color:blue;">Elite</mark>. Max level: `3`.
* **Hook** — Get more EXP from fishing. Applies to `Fishing Rod`. Rarity: <mark style="color:blue;">Elite</mark>. Max level: `3`.
* **Hook** — Chance to increase luck while fishing. Applies to `Fishing Rod`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `3`.
* **Poisoned Hook** — Poisons the hook. Applies to `Fishing Rod`. Rarity: <mark style="color:blue;">Elite</mark>. Max level: `3`.
* **Sharpness Hook** — Deal damage with the hook. Applies to `Fishing Rod`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `1`.
* **Snap** — Pull hit entity towards you. Applies to `Fishing Rod`. Rarity: <mark style="color:blue;">Elite</mark>. Max level: `3`.
