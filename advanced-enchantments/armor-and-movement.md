## <mark style="color:orange;">Armor</mark>

* **Angelic** — Heals health when damaged. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `5`.
* **Armored** — Decreases damage from enemy swords by `2%` per level. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:red;">Legendary</mark>. Max level: `4`.
* **Arrow Deflect** — Chance to prevent enemy arrows from dealing damage. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `4`.
* **Creeper Armor** — Chance to be immune to explosive damage. At higher levels you also have a chance to heal. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `3`.
* **Curse** — Chance to give your enemy mining fatigue. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:blue;">Elite</mark>. Max level: `2`.
* **Disappear** — Chance to become invisible when low on health. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `4`.
* **Divert** — Chance of poisoning your opponent and gaining regeneration. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:red;">Legendary</mark>. Max level: `5`.
* **Dodge** — Chance to dodge physical enemy damage. Chance increases when sneaking. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `5`.
* **Frozen** — Can cause slowness to the attacker when defending. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:blue;">Elite</mark>. Max level: `3`.
* **Guardians** — A chance to spawn iron golems to assist you and watch over you. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `10`.
* **Hardened** — Chance to recover durability when damaged by players. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:red;">Legendary</mark>. Max level: `3`.
* **Heavy** — Decreases damage from enemy bows by `2%` per level. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `5`.
* **Immortal** — Chance to prevent your armor from taking durability damage. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:purple;">Fabled</mark>. Max level: `4`.
* **Lucid** — Chance to cure blindness and gain night vision when hit. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:red;">Legendary</mark>. Max level: `3`.
* **Molten** — Chance of setting your attacker ablaze. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:green;">Unique</mark>. Max level: `4`.
* **Obsidianshield** — Gives permanent fire resistance. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `1`.
* **Overload** — Unlocks additional hearts. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:red;">Legendary</mark>. Max level: `3`.
* **Patch** — Chance to recover durability when damaged by mobs. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:red;">Legendary</mark>. Max level: `3`.
* **Poisoned** — Chance to give poison to your attacker. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:blue;">Elite</mark>. Max level: `4`.
* **Ragdoll** — Chance to be pushed back when getting hit. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `4`.
* **Reflect** — Absorb enemy damage and reflect it back. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:green;">Unique</mark>. Max level: `5`.
* **Safeguard** — Chance to gain Damage Resistance when defending. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `2`.
* **Spirits** — Chance to spawn guard blazes. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `10`.
* **Swordsman** — Chance to reduce incoming damage while wielding a sword by up to `22%` at max level. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `5`.
* **Tank** — Chance to decrease damage from enemy axes by `2%` per level. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:orange;">Ultimate</mark>. Max level: `4`.
* **Trickster** — When hit you have a chance to teleport directly behind your opponent. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:blue;">Elite</mark>. Max level: `8`.
* **Turmoil** — Chance to prevent opponents' guards from spawning. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:red;">Legendary</mark>. Max level: `3`.
* **Unholy** — Defend with weakness and wither effects. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:red;">Legendary</mark>. Max level: `5`.
* **Voodoo** — Gives a chance to deal weakness. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:blue;">Elite</mark>. Max level: `6`.
* **Ward** — A chance to absorb enemy damage. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:green;">Unique</mark>. Max level: `4`.
* **Wither** — A chance to give the wither effect. Applies to `Armor`. Rarity: <mark style="color:blue;">Elite</mark>. Max level: `5`.
